### Appliance components
* Raspberry Pi 3 Model B+
  * current latest
* Draws - Rasberry Pi HAT
  * High performance sound chip (CODEC)
  * GPS with pulse per second
  * 12V buck regulator
* Display
* Keyboard & mouse
